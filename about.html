How I Made This Site

There are four components to my setup: A USB camera, surveillance software, an upload script, and a webserver.

The camera I had lying around. It's a Logitech QuickCam 3000 Pro (the 3000 is for "cool"). The Linux driver can be found at http://www.saillard.org/linux/pwc/ which is distributed with Debian GNU/Linux.
